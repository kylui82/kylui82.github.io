Healthcare Data Report

SKILL
Data Analytic, Data Visualization, UI Design
TOOL
Tableau

Overview

The presented report was crafted as part of the Maven Analytics' Healthcare challenge (Related Link: https://mavenanalytics.io/challenges/maven-healthcare-challenge/26), a simulation where participants stepped into the shoes of a Data Analyst at the American Hospital Association (AHA). The AHA, a prominent national organization, plays a crucial role in representing hospitals and advocating for patients, serving as an authoritative source on healthcare matters and trends.


Objective

This task required a deep dive into the data, aiming to unearth insights and trends that could shed light on the impact of the survey on healthcare standards over the specified duration. By assuming the responsibilities of a Data Analyst for the AHA, participants engaged in a real-world scenario that demanded a keen analytical eye and an understanding of the dynamics influencing healthcare quality improvements.


Methodology

My methodology transitioned towards highlighting historical trends, presenting the present status of surveyed measures, and emphasizing weaknesses and opportunities for improvement. The emphasis was on deriving actionable insights rather than engaging in interaction, considering the latest survey results as the foundation for future enhancements. The report mainly incorporated Top-box Percentage, Middle-box Percentage and Bottom-box Percentage, representing the percentage of individuals rating their experience.
